Description:

During the pandemic in 2021, one of my friends decided to start her own sticker store with her sisters in hopes of empowering women in STEM through stickers which emphasized inclusivity in STEM areas and mental health.



I was asked to be the Director of Digital Media Marketing and was super excited to be a part of a small business that aligned with my values. Having little experience in the area, I researched market trends and social media analytics to plan the launching process and keep up with weekly contents/photoshoots/designs.



We launched on 6/11/21 at 3:14pm est and since then have done many customizations and commissions in addition to regular sales. Of course, there is much to be done and much more for me to learn, but I loved having the opportunity to contribute to such a wonderful cause!
