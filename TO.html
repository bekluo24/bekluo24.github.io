Emory ProgramHers

As a founding member of Emory ProgramHers, our mission is to form a supportive and helpful community for women in Computer Science and other technological fields at Emory through community building, mentorship, and professional development. We have managed the Grace Hopper Scholarship Program at Emory in addition to hosting panels and speaker series.
