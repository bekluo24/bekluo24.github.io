Girls Who Code (Communications Coordinator -> VP -> President -> Senior Advisor):

Emory Girls Who Code provides weekly lessons in Python and web development to middle and high school students in the Atlanta area. In the spring semester, students work to complete an impact project. We launched our first ever virtual school year 2020 – 2021 due to the pandemic.

Emory ProgramHers (VP -> President -> Senior Advisor):

As a founding member and senior advisor of Emory ProgramHers, our mission is to form a supportive and helpful community for women in Computer Science and other technological fields at Emory through community building, mentorship, and professional development. We have managed the Grace Hopper Scholarship Program at Emory in addition to hosting panels and speaker series.
