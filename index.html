ABOUT ME


Hello! I am currently a 4th year undergraduate student at Emory University double majoring in Computer Science and Economics with a concentration in Finance. I strive to explore various concentrations at the intersection of finance and tech to utilize data analytics, design principles, and research skills to solve real world problems.



I am extremely passionate about closing the gender gap in technological fields and improving environmental literacy amongst underrepresented student populations. In my free time, I love to draw, vlog, cook, and spend time with my cat Tofu!



This personal website was created as a side project for me to learn web design and at the same time organize my hobby portfolios as well as technical projects. Thank you for stopping by and feel free to reach out!
