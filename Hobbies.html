Food

Cooking is my newest hobby. I love how therapeutic the process of planning and prepping for a meal is. The best part is of course eating the food afterwards. I never follow a recipe completely as I like to put my own touch and creativity into each meal. I am very much still a beginner but perhaps cooking for my roommates this year will allow me to improve!
